About NeuroScan AI

NeuroScan AI is an online, browser-based entertainment platform designed to provide users with a fun and engaging lie detector style game. The website was created to offer a simple interactive experience that can be enjoyed without registration, downloads, or personal data collection.

This website does not perform real lie detection, psychological analysis, or biometric scanning. All results generated by the game are random and intended solely for entertainment purposes. Users should not rely on the results for making personal, professional, or legal decisions.

Our goal is to build lightweight and accessible web games that work smoothly across desktop and mobile devices. We prioritize transparency, user trust, and compliance with online content guidelines.

If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, feel free to reach out through our Contact page.
